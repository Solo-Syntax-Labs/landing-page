Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about how we partner, build, and deliver AI software.

What’s the typical timeline from idea to MVP?

Most projects follow: 1–2 weeks discovery, 2–4 weeks prototype, and 4–8 weeks to production depending on scope and integrations. We share a clear plan with milestones after discovery.

Can you work with our existing stack and cloud provider?

Yes. We build with Python, TypeScript/Node, and modern web stacks; deploy to AWS, Azure, GCP, or on‑prem. We containerize services and use infrastructure‑as‑code for repeatable environments.

Do you use foundation models or build custom LLMs?

Both. We evaluate build vs. buy: start with best‑fit models (OpenAI, Claude, local LLMs) and fine‑tune or design custom models when it improves accuracy, latency, cost, or privacy.

How do you handle data security, privacy, and IP?

Your data stays in your tenant whenever possible. We follow least‑privilege access, encrypt data in transit/at rest, and sign NDAs. IP produced for your project is assigned to you per contract.

What engagement models and budgets do you support?

Options include fixed‑scope sprints and monthly product pods. Typical MVPs land in the $50K–$150K range depending on complexity. We provide transparent estimates and weekly progress.

What happens after launch?

We offer handover, documentation, training, CI/CD setup, monitoring, and optional support SLAs. We can continue iterating on features or help your team take full ownership.
